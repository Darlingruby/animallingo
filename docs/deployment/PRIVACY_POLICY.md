# Privacy Policy - AnimalLingo

**Last Updated:** March 1, 2026

## Overview
AnimalLingo respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data We Collect

### Audio Data
- **What:** Short audio clips of pet vocalizations
- **Why:** To analyze and translate pet communication
- **Storage:** Processed on-device when possible; encrypted cloud storage for training
- **Retention:** 30 days maximum; deleted immediately upon request

### User Account
- **What:** Email, name, pet profiles
- **Why:** To save translations and personalize experience
- **Storage:** Encrypted PostgreSQL database

### Usage Analytics
- **What:** Feature usage, error logs, performance metrics
- **Why:** To improve app performance
- **Storage:** Anonymized; no personal identifiers

## Data We Don't Collect
- ❌ Video from camera (unless explicitly enabled for reptile vibration mode)
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Contacts
- ❌ Health information
- ❌ Pheromone data (impossible on mobile devices)

## Permissions Required

### Microphone
- **Purpose:** Record pet vocalizations for translation
- **Optional:** No; required for core functionality

### Camera
- **Purpose:** Analyze body language and reptile vibrations
- **Optional:** Yes; only needed for visual analysis features

### Storage
- **Purpose:** Save translation history locally
- **Optional:** Yes; cloud sync available

## Third-Party Services
- Firebase (analytics, crash reporting)
- Stripe (payment processing)
- Cloud providers (AWS/GCP for ML inference)

## Your Rights
- Access your data
- Delete your account and data
- Export translation history
- Opt out of analytics

## Accuracy Disclaimer
This app provides estimated translations based on acoustic analysis. Results (60-85% accuracy) vary by species and individual pet. Not a substitute for professional animal behavior consultation.
