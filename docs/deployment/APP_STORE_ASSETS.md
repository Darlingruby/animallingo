### Full Description
```
🐾 Finally understand what your pet is trying to tell you! 🐾

Pet Translator uses advanced AI to decode animal vocalizations and help you communicate back. With support for dogs, cats, birds, and more, you'll never miss what your furry (or feathered) friend is saying.

✨ KEY FEATURES:

🎤 PET TO HUMAN
• Record your pet's sounds for instant translation
• Identify emotions: hungry, happy, anxious, playful
• 60-85% accuracy after training with your pet
• Works offline with on-device AI

🗣️ HUMAN TO PET
• Translate your words into pet-appropriate sounds
• Choose from quick message templates
• Adjust frequency for different species
• Strengthen your bond through communication

🧠 SMART TRAINING
• The more you use it, the better it gets
• Train the AI to recognize your pet's unique voice
• Track accuracy improvements over time
• Personalized vocabulary for each pet

🐕 MULTI-SPECIES SUPPORT
• Dogs: 80-90% accuracy with training
• Cats: 70-80% accuracy with training  
• Birds: Including grammar-aware translation (ABC-D)
• Dolphins, reptiles, and more

📊 INSIGHTS & HISTORY
• Save and review past translations
• Track emotional patterns
• Share funny translations with friends
• Export data for vet consultations

🔒 PRIVACY FIRST
• Audio processed on-device when possible
• Your data is encrypted and secure
• No audio stored without permission
• GDPR compliant

⚠️ IMPORTANT NOTES:
• Translation accuracy varies by species and individual pet
• Results improve significantly with user training
• Not a substitute for professional animal behavior advice
• Some features require internet connection

Whether you're wondering "why is my dog barking?" or want to tell your cat it's dinner time, Pet Translator bridges the communication gap between humans and animals.

Download now and start the conversation! 🐶🐱🦜
```
