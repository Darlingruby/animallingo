# App Store Deployment Guide - AnimalLingo

## Apple App Store (iOS)

### Prerequisites
- Apple Developer Account ($99/year)
- macOS with Xcode 15+
- App Store Connect access

### Build Process
1. Configure signing certificates
2. Archive build in Xcode
3. Upload via Transporter or Xcode
4. Submit for review

### Required Assets
- App Icon: 1024x1024 PNG
- Screenshots: iPhone (6.5" + 5.5") + iPad (12.9")
- App Preview Video (optional)
- Privacy Policy URL
- Support URL

### App Store Optimization (ASO)
- Title: AnimalLingo - Talk to Animals
- Subtitle: Decode animal sounds & communicate back
- Keywords: animal translator, pet translator, dog translator, cat translator, bird translator, animal communication, animallingo
- Description: Highlight bidirectional translation, 60-85% accuracy, 6+ species

### Review Guidelines Compliance
- No health claims (translation accuracy only)
- Clear subscription pricing
- Privacy nutrition label for microphone access
- No mention of "100% accurate" or "guaranteed"

## Samsung Galaxy Store (Android)

### Prerequisites
- Samsung Developer Account (free)
- Android App Bundle (AAB) signed

### Build Process
1. Generate signed AAB in Android Studio
2. Upload to Samsung Seller Portal
3. Complete content rating questionnaire
4. Submit for review

### Required Assets
- App Icon: 512x512 PNG
- Screenshots: Phone (16:9 or 9:16)
- Feature Graphic: 1024x500 JPG/PNG
- App Description (4000 char max)

### Galaxy Store Optimization
- Title: Pet Translator - Animal Communication
- Tagline: Understand what your pet is saying
- Category: Lifestyle > Pets
- Keywords: pet, dog, cat, translator, animal, communication

---

## Google Play Store (Android)

### Prerequisites
- Google Play Developer Account ($25 one-time)
- Google Play Console access

### Build Process
1. Generate signed AAB
2. Create release in Play Console
3. Upload AAB and assets
4. Complete store listing
5. Submit for review

### Required Assets
- App Icon: 512x512 PNG
- Feature Graphic: 1024x500 PNG
- Screenshots: Phone + 7" tablet + 10" tablet
- Promo Video (optional)
